Main Goal

  • Normal Siege: defend the center base from enemies coming from all sides.
  • Stage 1–50: survive all 50 stages to win the campaign.
  • Endless: keeps going after the normal game and scales harder forever.
  • Gold: used during a run to place towers, farms, upgrades, and specials.
  • XP: permanent account currency used for upgrade paths, VIP, generators, Boss Siege upgrades, and trades.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys: move camera.
  • Mouse click / drag: place soldiers or walls depending on selected mode.
  • Click: place farms, outposts, or airships.
  • Upgrade buttons: upgrade the nearest valid object to your mouse.
  • Save & Exit: saves your normal Siege run and returns to menu.

Basic Defenses

  • Soldiers: cheap basic attackers. They shoot nearby enemies.
  • Walls: cheap blockers that slow enemy movement toward the base.
  • Farms: produce gold over time and become very important in long runs.
  • Outposts: moving defense platforms with multiple soldiers inside.
  • Airships: orbit the base and attack enemies from range.

Soldier Upgrades

  • Tank: slower, tougher, bigger hits, splash damage.
  • Flamethrower: short range but fast area damage.
  • Machine Gun: fast shooting and good range.

Structure Upgrades

  • Outposts: Mega Post, Swift Outpost, Shield Convoy.
  • Farms: Printer, Smart Gold, Supply Drop.
  • Airships: Ravager, Spewer, Carrier.
  • These upgrades affect the nearest matching basic structure to your mouse.

Special Map Upgrades

  • Black Holes: pulls and damages enemies.
  • Smart Missiles: launches missiles at enemies.
  • Air Strike: drops bombs across the map.
  • Heaven’s Strike: strong beam damage in dense enemy groups.
  • Forcefield: pulsing base-wide damage ring.
  • Voltizer: zaps enemies for percentage damage.
  • Radiator: radiation cloud and explosion damage.

XP Upgrade Paths

  • Soldier Path: improves soldier damage, stats, and regeneration.
  • Outpost Path: improves outpost speed, health, and survivability.
  • Farm Path: improves farm income and farm upgrade value.
  • Special Path: improves special damage and special costs.
  • Airship Path: improves airship damage, stun, and shields.
  • Sabotage Path: weakens enemies and makes them take more damage.
  • Base Path: improves base HP and gives a strong defense aura near base.

VIP

  • VIP Unlock: expensive permanent unlock using XP.
  • Instant Place: free placements using recharge charges.
  • Instant Upgrade: free upgrades using recharge charges.
  • Instant Bucks: optional starting gold bonus using charges.

Generators

  • XP Production Site: generates XP over time.
  • Trophy Factory: generates trophies over time.
  • Upgrade generators with XP to improve long-term progression.

Boss World

  • Boss World is a separate boss game inside the menu.
  • You select a boss, fight towers/enemies, and earn Boss XP.
  • Rush Wave is inside Boss World only.
  • Bosses level up and prestige in Boss World.

Boss Siege

  • Unlocks when a boss is fully maxed/prestiged and ascended.
  • You play as the boss inside Siege survival.
  • No base. Enemies target you instead.
  • Only farms and boss upgrades/specials are allowed.
  • Boss Siege upgrades use normal XP and scale to 1.4M total XP.

World Bosses

  • BarkBarian: giant tree monster with roots and regeneration.
  • Lumina Dyra: lightning dragon with storm attacks.
  • Dystopiatos: unnatural void monster with powerful attacks.
  • World Bosses now spawn into the actual Siege game area, not just a menu bar.
  • Your towers, airships, and specials damage the active World Boss over time.
  • Deal 1,000,000 lifetime damage to earn that boss’s trophy animation.

Relics

  • There are 60 relics.
  • Relics boost stats like XP, Boss XP, gold, HP, damage, farms, specials, attack speed, and harbor value.
  • Equip up to 5 relics at once.
  • Relics come from World Boss rewards and Trade Harbor.

Trade Harbor

  • Select an item, amount, and desired item to preview an exchange.
  • Trade XP, Boss XP, gold, trophies, season badges, and relic dust.
  • Harbor offers refresh over time and can sell relics or special exchanges.
  • Harbor-value relics improve trade payouts.

Collection Book

  • Tracks relics, World Boss trophies, and badge progress.
  • Completion milestones give XP, badges, and relics.
  • Use it as the long-term checklist for the whole game.

Trophy Room

  • Shows World Boss trophies.
  • Shows season badges and achievement badges.
  • Locked trophies appear dim until earned.
  • World Boss trophies are earned from 1,000,000 lifetime damage.
