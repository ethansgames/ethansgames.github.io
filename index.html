Site Information

This website is a project made by me (also known as Maxie) as a public repository of all of my brother Ethan's games. As of 6/26/2026, Ethan has made 18 whole html games, with varying qualities like size (largest is 7.351 MB!) and dimensions (three officially finished 3D games for now). Of course, he makes the games and I create, modify, and maintain the website. All games are licensed to me by him with permission, and you can find the general game license (license for all games unless specified otherwise for a particular game) by clicking here or opening games/LICENSE.txt.

Who Makes What?

My brother Ethan makes the html games, and I (GrepBDS) upload them to this repository and make them availible for everyone to play. I also maintain the website and add new features to it.

How Do I Contact You?

You can contact me by sending an email to 'ILoveComputerMan@protonmail.com'. I should respond within a few business days.

Why Is The License So Restrictive? Will You Switch To a More Open-source License?

Unfortunately, I will not be loosening the restrictions for now, as we have not decided what license to release the games under, and it is simply easier to move to a less restrictive license compared to doing vice versa. In the near or far future, we could move to a different license, so be sure to look out for that!
