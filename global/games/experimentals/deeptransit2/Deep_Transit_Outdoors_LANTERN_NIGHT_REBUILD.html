DEEP TRANSIT: OUTDOORS

A 15,000 meter rainy night trail lit almost entirely by warm lanterns. Follow the winding path through garden arches, iron fences, ruins, sheds, and closet huts.

Mandrake: camouflaged holes beside the route. If it latches on, follow the vine and put it back.

Surge: yellow sky sparks warn you. Hide in a closet before it rushes from behind.
